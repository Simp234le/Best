veding machine

(This project will requires 2 laptops) Our project is the “vending machine” which is a miniature version of the real one, due to the lack of components we were limited to only our hands for it to work. The vending machine consists of only cardboard and the micro bit components, but for it to work we will need to have a code, the code we have came up with is via javascript;

  • step 1

    We then turn the cardboard vertically then “using a boxcutter” to create a rectangular hole in the top center of the cardboard horizontally, which is the coin slot for the machine. After that we make an area to fit our Ultrasonic sensor in, by measuring the length and width of the component we effectively make a spot to slide the component in.

  • step 2

    Next we make a hole 4 cm down from the original hole and cut a rectangular hole matching the dimensions of the microbit, this is the area of which our 1st microbit is placed, fold the net into a rectangular prism and tape on all sides to secure the prism. Following that we create another net ⅓ taller in height and ½ wider in width. Subsequently we make a hole measuring the same dimensions of the micro bit 2cm down from the top of the net, center vertically.

  • step 3

    Then we create a much bigger hole in the middle of the cardboard 2cm down from the previous hole and 2 cm from both of the sides, make sure to also make the hole 7 cm from the bottom. Next we cut open a hole 2 cm down from the biggest hole, 2 cm from the sides and 1 cm from the bottom, this is the opening for the items in the vending machine. On the back, create a hole exactly in the center of the face and poke a stick through it, this is the hinge for the handle. Make a ledge 4 cm from the hole we had just made. This is the place where we put our 2nd microbit component. Slide a cardboard piece 4 cm down the stick, anchor it to the stick.

  • step 4

    Make a cardboard piece matching the top part of the net, make a hole in the center of the piece. To ensure the items fall on top of the stick piece. line the hole above the stick making sure it is 1 cm above. Fold the net into place creating a rectangular prism. Place the micro bit components in the areas stated in this instruction and connect them from the microbit to a battery pack or a computer and sticky tape them on Lastly, make the items you want to drop from the microbit make sure to make them smaller than the hole above the stick. Download the code via “Make code” on the bottom left of your screen after you finished with code #1 This code is for the coin slot. Take your 2nd laptop and Download Via the same website, for Code #2 This code is for the stick handle.
